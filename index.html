IELTS-style Speaking Practice – Units 1–4

Short Part 1-style questions (about 15–25 seconds each).

Choose your unit, then answer the three questions in the prompt. Try to speak naturally and clearly.

Prompt 1 – Animals & Pets (Unit 1)
  1. Do you have any pets? If yes, what are they like? If not, would you like to have one?
  2. What animals are common in your country? Where do people usually see them?
  3. Do you think it is better to see animals in zoos or in the wild? Why?
Prompt 2 – The Environment (Unit 2)
  1. Is the air and water in your city generally clean or dirty? Can you give an example?
  2. What small things can people do every day to help the environment?
  3. Do you think young people care more about the environment than older people? Why or why not?
